FADE IN:

EXT. MOUNTAIN PEAK - DAWN

A lone figure stands atop a snow-capped mountain, watching the sunrise paint the valley in shades of gold and crimson. This is SARAH (28), dressed in climbing gear, her face determined yet weary.

SARAH
(voiceover)
They said it was impossible. They said no one could reach the summit of Mount Aether. But they forgot one thing... I'm not just anyone.

Sarah checks her equipment - a state-of-the-art climbing harness and a mysterious glowing device strapped to her wrist.

EXT. MOUNTAIN PEAK - MOMENTS LATER

The device on Sarah's wrist begins to beep rapidly. A holographic map projects from it, showing a complex network of tunnels inside the mountain.

SARAH
(whispering)
So it's true... the Aetherium Core really exists.

Suddenly, the ground begins to shake. The snow around Sarah starts to crack and fall away, revealing a hidden entrance beneath.

INT. MOUNTAIN TUNNEL - CONTINUOUS

Sarah descends into the darkness. The tunnel walls are lined with strange crystals that pulse with blue light.

SARAH
Wow... this technology is beyond anything I've ever seen.

As she ventures deeper, she hears footsteps echoing behind her.

SARAH
(tensing)
Who's there?

From the shadows emerges PROFESSOR MICHAELS (60s), a distinguished scientist with wild white hair and excited eyes.

PROFESSOR MICHAELS
Miss Chen! I knew you'd make it. The Aetherium Core has chosen you.

SARAH
Professor? What are you doing here? And what do you mean "chosen me"?

PROFESSOR MICHAELS
The Core is an ancient alien artifact. It doesn't just show the way - it chooses who's worthy to wield its power.

The professor leads Sarah to a massive cavern. In the center floats a crystalline sphere, radiating immense energy.

SARAH
Incredible... This could change everything.

PROFESSOR MICHAELS
Indeed. But there are others who want it for themselves. We don't have much time.

Suddenly, alarms blare. Red lights flash throughout the cavern.

PROFESSOR MICHAELS
They're here! The corporation's mercenaries! You must take the Core and escape!

SARAH
But what about you?

PROFESSOR MICHAELS
Some things are worth sacrificing for. Now go! Save the future!

Sarah hesitates, then reaches out and touches the Aetherium Core. It immediately bonds with her wrist device, creating a brilliant flash of light.

SARAH
I won't let you down.

With newfound determination, Sarah turns and runs toward the tunnel entrance as armed mercenaries storm the cavern.

FADE OUT.

THE END
